Mera
Vault

Mera Vault is aimed at providing a much needed facility of storing a confidential document and only allowing trusted users of an organisation to access it hereby keeping the secrecy of the document. It helps document to get stored in a safe place only permitting authorised users to access it and prevent any form of unauthorised use.
